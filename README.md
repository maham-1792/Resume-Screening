The Resume Screening System supports PDF, DOCX, and TXT files. It allows users to upload their resumes and provides the following functionalities:

1. **Resume Categorization**
2. **Job Recommendations**
3. **Information Extraction**

## Features

- **Resume Categorization**: Categorizes the resume into predefined categories.
- **Job Recommendations**: Provides job recommendations based on the resume content.
- **Information Extraction**: Extracts key information such as name, phone number, email, skills, and education.

## How to Use
**Upload Resume:** Click on the 'Upload' button to select and upload your resume file.
**View Category:** The system will categorize the resume based on its content.
**Get Job Recommendation:** The system will suggest a suitable job role.
**View Parsed Information:** Extracted information such as name, phone number, email, skills, and education will be displayed.
